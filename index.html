Definition

What is water
pollution?

Water pollution can be defined as the contamination of a stream, river,
lake, ocean or any other stretch of water, depleting water quality and
making it toxic for the environment and humans.

There are two types of water pollution:
  1. Organic pollution due to microorganisms - bacteria and viruses -
    present in the water, generated by excrement, animal and
    vegetable waste
  2. Chemical pollution generated by the nitrates and phosphates of
    pesticides, human and animal drugs, household products, heavy
    metals, acids and hydrocarbons used in industries
